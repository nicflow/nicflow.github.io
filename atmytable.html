Eats

One of my most favorite things in this world in cooking for friends and once or twice a week a I host the San Francisco Supper Series to bring friends who don't know each other, together. Though it's also one of my favorite creative outlets, I hardly do it just for me because I'd be eating the same meal for 17 days, you guys! The Supper Series, a tradition started by my college roommates, is a great way to forge new community as well as practice my culinary (read: how to follow a recipe) skills.

There are a couple of things you should know in the event you don't ever make it to my kitchen. First, I always cook to Soft Rock. Specifically, my Christopher Cross Pandora station. To aid you in your culinary pursuits, I've created this dreamy Spotify playlist with some of my all-time favorites just for you! Please freely enjoy Lionel, Phil, and England Dan for days to come.

Second, I do not bake and I don't do breakfast. Sandwiches are my favorite for lunch, well paired with some fruit and veg. I don't like eating lunch items before noon and have a hard time with dinner for lunch (you know what I mean) and therefore, we'll just stick to dinner recipes, whaddaya say?

Anyway, let's get to it! Here are some of my most tried and true recipes. Hope you get to enjoy one or two with your tribe soon!
